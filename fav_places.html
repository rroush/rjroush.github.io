My Favorite Places

The following is a list of places ranked according to my preference. Some of these locations I have been to and many of them I have yet to visit. Each location will include a short description with an explanation of why I chose it, and they are given in no particular order. Please enjoy my favorite places and I hope you find them inspiring!



London, England

The city of London is the capital of England. It is located on the River Thames. My favorite feature of the city is the variety of museums. Near London is the Natural History Museum, the Museum of London, the British Museum, the Churchill War Rooms, and the Victoria and Albert Museum just to name a few. The city also has many fantastic dining options that can easily be reached by the public transit system.

Follow this link to find out more about the museums of London.

Seven Sisters Springs, Florida (USA)

Florida is home to many beautiful places to get into the water, but Seven Sisters Springs is my absolute favorite. These springs are a natural inland waterway that branch off the Chassahowitzka River. It is a manatee and otter gathering place. At this location, visitors can kayak or paddle board the river as well as snorkel at The Crack, and underwater cliff edge.

Here is a post that tells more about the hidden beauties of Seven Sister Springs.



Florence, Italy

Florence is within the Italian region of Tuscany. It is known for its historical roots dating back to medieval times which is still seen in the city's architecture. The city also has many delicious restaurants that showcase true Italian food.

Great Barrier Reef, Australia

The Great Barrier Reef is the world's biggest reef system. It is located off of the coast of Queensland, Australia in the Coral Sea. Snorkeling is the most common activity by visitors in order to see the beauty of the reefs and appreciate the sealife that lives in the area.

Click here to learn more about the Great Barrier Reefs

St.Lucia, Caribbean Islands

St.Lucia is one of the islands in the eastern Caribbean Sea. It is the essential island getaway and has many beautiful sights. The island also has many cultural festivals.
